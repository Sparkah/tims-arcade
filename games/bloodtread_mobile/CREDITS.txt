Bloodtread - third-party asset credits
========================================

Most third-party art below is CC0 (Public Domain, no attribution required).
EXCEPTION: the LPC ENEMY SPRITES (bottom section, 2026-06-24) are LPC-format = CC-BY-SA 3.0 / GPL,
which REQUIRE attribution + share-alike (NOT CC0). Recorded here for provenance.
