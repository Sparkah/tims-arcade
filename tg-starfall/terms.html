Starfall Sprint Terms

Last updated: June 24, 2026.

Digital items

Starfall Sprint sells optional digital items inside the Telegram Mini App using Telegram Stars. Items are delivered in the game after Telegram reports the invoice as paid.

Refunds and support

Digital items are normally final once delivered, except where applicable law or Telegram policy requires otherwise. For purchase help, open @gamesfactorybot and send /paysupport with the payment problem and approximate purchase time.

Ads

Rewarded ads are optional. Rewards are granted after the ad provider reports completion. If ads are unavailable, the game may show a test reward fallback while the Mini App is being configured.

Local save

Progress is stored in the browser or Telegram WebView local storage on your device. Clearing app data may reset local progress.

Contact

Payment support: @gamesfactorybot via /paysupport. General support: @gamesfactorybot via /support.
