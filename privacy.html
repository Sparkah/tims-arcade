Privacy Policy

game-factory.tech - last updated 27 May 2026

game-factory.tech is an independent studio that builds and publishes free HTML5 browser games. This policy explains what information we handle, including for the tool we use to publish our own promotional videos to our own YouTube channel.

Our video-publishing tool and the YouTube API

We operate a private, internal tool ("video-factory") that uploads short promotional gameplay clips of our own games to our own YouTube channel. This tool uses YouTube API Services.

  • It authorizes to a single Google/YouTube account that we own, using the OAuth scope https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.upload (upload only).
  • It is used only to upload our own videos. It does not read, collect, store, or share any other YouTube user's data, channel data, watch history, or personal information.
  • OAuth tokens are stored locally on our own machine with restricted file permissions and are never shared with any third party.

YouTube API Services - required disclosures

This tool uses YouTube API Services. By extension:

  • Your use of YouTube is subject to the YouTube Terms of Service.
  • Our handling of any information obtained through YouTube API Services adheres to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • You can review and revoke our tool's access to your Google account at any time via the Google security settings page.

Information collected on game-factory.tech

When you play games on our site we may record anonymous, aggregated engagement signals (such as plays and votes) to understand which games people enjoy, and any game ideas you voluntarily submit through the site. We do not require an account to play, and we do not sell personal data.

Data sharing and retention

We do not sell or rent personal information. We retain only what is needed for the purposes above and remove data that is no longer needed. We may disclose information if required by law.

Children

Our games are general-audience and not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
