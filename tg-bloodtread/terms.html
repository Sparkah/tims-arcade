Bloodtread Terms

Last updated: June 24, 2026.

Digital items

Bloodtread sells optional digital items inside the Telegram Mini App using Telegram Stars or TON. Items add in-game blood, hull and arsenal upgrades, ad-free play, or the Bloodgod Pact, and are delivered only after the purchase is confirmed paid by the backend receipt.

Refunds and support

Digital items are normally final once delivered, except where applicable law or Telegram policy requires otherwise. For purchase help, open @gamesfactorybot and send /paysupport with the payment problem and approximate purchase time.

Ads

Rewarded ads are optional. Rewards are granted only after the ad provider reports completion. If rewarded ads are unavailable, Bloodtread does not grant the extra rewarded-ad bonus.

Local save

Progress is stored in the browser or Telegram WebView local storage on your device. Clearing app data may reset local progress.

Contact

Payment support: @gamesfactorybot via /paysupport. General support: @gamesfactorybot via /support.
