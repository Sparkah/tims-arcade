Megaton Terms

Last updated: June 24, 2026.

Digital items

Megaton sells optional digital items inside the Telegram Mini App using Telegram Stars. Items add in-game caps or warhead upgrades and are delivered after Telegram reports the invoice as paid.

Refunds and support

Digital items are normally final once delivered, except where applicable law or Telegram policy requires otherwise. For purchase help, open @gamesfactorybot and send /paysupport with the payment problem and approximate purchase time.

Ads

Rewarded ads are optional. Rewards are granted only after the ad provider reports completion. If rewarded ads are unavailable, Megaton does not grant the extra rewarded-ad bonus.

Local save

Progress is stored in the browser or Telegram WebView local storage on your device. Clearing app data may reset local progress.

Contact

Payment support: @gamesfactorybot via /paysupport. General support: @gamesfactorybot via /support.
